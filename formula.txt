Money Flow Multiplier = ((Close value – Low value) – (High value – Close value)) / (High value – Low value)

MFM = ((1851.800049-1792.099976)-(1858-1851.800049))/(1858-1792.099976)

	= ((59.700073)-(6.199951))/(65.900024)
	
	= (53.500122)/(65.900024)

	= 0.811838

Money Flow Volume = Money Flow Multiplier x Volume for the Period

MFV = 0.811838 x 21697962
	
	= 17610359.05

CMF = 21-day Average of the Daily Money Flow / 21-day Average of the Volume

	= 17610359.05 / 21697962
